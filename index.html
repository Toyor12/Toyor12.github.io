About Me

Who I Am

I am a Data Engineer and Analyst with 3+ years of experience designing scalable data pipelines, Power BI dashboards and automated reporting solutions across Finance, Supply Chain and Commercial environments in Germany and beyond.

My work spans the full data lifecycle — ingesting raw data with PySpark and AWS Glue, transforming it with dbt and SQL, deploying ML models with FastAPI and Kubernetes, to surfacing insights through Power BI dashboards with DAX time intelligence.

I am experienced working with German-speaking stakeholders and cross-functional teams, having delivered analytics solutions at Wilkinson Sword GmbH, Dr. Eckel Animal Nutrition and Shyft Management Consulting in Germany. I hold a certified SAP S/4HANA and Microsoft Azure (AZ-900) background.
